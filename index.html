Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Serving Size: 8 servings

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add milk, oil, eggs, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour into greased pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans to cool completely.
